# Audience Dimensions

Understanding your audience is crucial for effective hooks.

## Demographic Dimensions

*   **Age:** Different age groups respond to different appeals.
*   **Gender:** Gender can influence interests and priorities.
*   **Location:** Geographical location can impact cultural references and needs.
*   **Income Level:** Affordability and perceived value vary with income.

## Psychographic Dimensions

*   **Interests:** What hobbies, passions, and topics engage them?
*   **Values:** What principles and beliefs guide their decisions?
*   **Pain Points:** What problems or frustrations do they experience?
*   **Aspirations:** What are their goals, dreams, and desires?
