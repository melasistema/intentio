# Flux Token Amplifiers for Cartoon Universe

This document contains rules for amplifying tokens to increase their impact in the latent space of diffusion models like Flux, specifically tailored for enforcing the consistent "Cartoon Universe" style.

## I. Core Style Feature Amplifiers

-   **When `style` = `cartoon`:**
    -   **Amplified:** `vibrant cartoon art style, bold clean outlines, flat colors, cel-shaded, exaggerated proportions, graphic illustration`

-   **When `subject` = `character`:**
    -   **Amplified:** `expressive cartoon character, simplified anatomy, large expressive eyes, clear readable pose`

-   **When `action` is dynamic:**
    -   **Amplified:** `dynamic cartoon pose, action lines, speed lines, exaggerated motion blur (stylized)`

## II. Visual Grammar Amplifiers

-   **Line Art Amplification:**
    -   **Standard:** `bold lines`
    -   **Amplified:** `uniform medium-heavy line weight, crisp clean outlines, solid opaque lines, graphic line art`

-   **Color Amplification:**
    -   **Standard:** `bright colors`
    -   **Amplified:** `vibrant flat color fills, limited color palette, highly saturated cartoon colors`

-   **Shading Amplification:**
    -   **Standard:** `cel shading`
    -   **Amplified:** `hard-edged cel shading, distinct shadow shapes, graphic highlights, no gradients`

-   **Proportion Amplification:**
    -   **Standard:** `exaggerated proportions`
    -   **Amplified:** `large head small body ratio, simplified limbs, expressive facial features, consistent cartoon anatomy`

## III. Environmental Amplifiers

-   **When `background` = `simple`:**
    -   **Amplified:** `flat color background, graphic elements, simplified cartoon environment, clear layered depth`

-   **When `object` is inanimate:**
    -   **Amplified:** `simplified cartoon object, clean graphic form, bold outlines, flat shaded`
