# 2026 Social Media Platform Specifications

## 1. LinkedIn (Authority & Professional Growth)
* **The "See More" Cutoff:** * **Mobile:** 140 characters.
    * **Desktop:** ~210 characters.
* **Optimal Post Length:** 1,200 to 2,500 characters for "Deep Value" content.
* **2026 Algorithm Signal:** Favors "Conversation Depth." Comments over 5 words from users in your industry boost reach by 3x.
* **Hook Rule:** Must address a professional pain point or share a contrarian industry insight in the first 140 characters.
