# Risk Assessment Principles

This document outlines principles for effective risk assessment in decision-making.

## Key Concepts

*   **Risk Identification:** Systematically identifying potential positive and negative deviations from expected outcomes.
*   **Risk Analysis:** Understanding the nature of the risk, its potential consequences, and the likelihood of its occurrence.
*   **Risk Evaluation:** Comparing the estimated risks against risk criteria to determine the significance of the risk.
*   **Risk Treatment:** Implementing actions to modify the risks (e.g., avoid, reduce, share, retain).
*   **Monitoring and Review:** Continuously tracking risks, reviewing risk treatment effectiveness, and identifying new risks.

## Categories of Risk

*   **Financial Risk:** Impact on monetary resources.
*   **Operational Risk:** Impact on day-to-day activities and processes.
*   **Strategic Risk:** Impact on long-term goals and objectives.
*   **Reputational Risk:** Impact on public perception and brand image.
*   **Compliance Risk:** Impact of failing to adhere to laws, regulations, or internal policies.
