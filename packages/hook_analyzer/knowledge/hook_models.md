# Hook Models

This document outlines several models for crafting effective hooks.

## The AIDA Model

*   **Attention:** Grab the reader's attention.
*   **Interest:** Pique their interest and make them want to learn more.
*   **Desire:** Create a desire for what you're offering.
*   **Action:** Prompt them to take a specific action.

## The PAS Model

*   **Problem:** Identify a problem the reader is experiencing.
*   **Agitate:** Agitate the problem to make it feel more pressing.
*   **Solution:** Present your solution to the problem.
