# Platform Adapter Generator

## Instructions:
1. **Input:** Receive a "Neutral Hook" and a "Target Platform" (LinkedIn, X, or Instagram).
2. **Logic Mapping:**
    * **LinkedIn:** Focus on *Professional Stakes*. Use "The Case Study" or "The Hard Truth" angle. Ensure there is a "See More" gap at 210 characters.
    * **X (Twitter):** Focus on *High Density*. Use "The Listicle" or "The Contrarian" angle. Keep it under 280 characters.
    * **Instagram:** Focus on *Relatability*. Use "The POV" or "The Secret Tip" angle.
3. **Output:** Provide 3 variations of the hook tailored specifically for that platform's audience mindset.
