# Cognitive Biases

This document lists cognitive biases that can be leveraged in hooks.

*   **Confirmation Bias:** People tend to favor information that confirms their existing beliefs.
*   **Fear of Missing Out (FOMO):** People are motivated to act by the fear that they will miss out on an opportunity.
*   **Anchoring Bias:** People are overly influenced by the first piece of information they receive.
*   **Bandwagon Effect:** People are more likely to do something if they see others doing it.
