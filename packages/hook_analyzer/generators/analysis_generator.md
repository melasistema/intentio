# Analysis Generator

This generator is designed to perform a detailed analysis of a given "hook" or piece of persuasive text.

## Instructions:

1.  **Input:** Receive a "hook" from the user.
2.  **Context:** Utilize the knowledge base on `hook_models.md`, `cognitive_biases.md`, `audience_dimensions.md`, and `evaluation_rules.md`.
3.  **Process:**
    *   Break down the hook into its core components.
    *   Assess its clarity and attention-grabbing potential.
    *   Identify any leveraged cognitive biases.
    *   Evaluate its relevance to potential audience dimensions.
    *   Apply the evaluation rules to determine strengths and weaknesses.
4.  **Output:** Provide a structured analysis that explains *why* the hook works or fails, citing specific principles from the knowledge base. Do not rewrite the hook; focus solely on the analysis.
