# Risk Analysis Generator

This generator focuses on performing a detailed risk analysis for a given decision or plan.

## Instructions:

1.  **Input:** Receive a description of a decision, project, or plan, including its objectives and proposed actions.
2.  **Context:** Utilize the knowledge base on `risk_assessment.md`, `cognitive_biases_decision.md`, and potentially `decision_frameworks.md` for context on decision structure.
3.  **Process:**
    *   Identify potential risks (positive and negative) associated with the input.
    *   Analyze the likelihood and potential impact of each identified risk.
    *   Categorize risks (e.g., financial, operational, strategic, reputational).
    *   Suggest potential mitigation strategies or contingency plans for significant risks.
4.  **Output:** Provide a structured risk analysis report that lists identified risks, assesses their severity, and proposes ways to manage them.
